Our Trip Packages

Family Float Adventure

Difficulty: Beginner | Duration: Half Day (3-4 hours) Ideal For: Families, Beginners, First-Time Rafters

Overview

Experience a gentle, scenic journey along the river’s calmer sections, perfect for families and first-time rafters. Our Family Float Adventure combines mild rapids with plenty of smooth, relaxing stretches, allowing guests to soak in the beautiful river valley views without the intense thrill of larger rapids. Our expert guides will share fascinating facts about the area’s wildlife, geology, and the river’s history, making this a fun, educational trip that everyone can enjoy.

Highlights

  • Mild, beginner-friendly rapids for a safe, fun experience
  • Beautiful, untouched scenery ideal for nature lovers
  • Stops for swimming, riverside picnics, and photo ops
  • Knowledgeable guides who bring the river’s ecosystem and history to life

Whether you’re looking to bond with family or introduce friends to rafting, our Family Float Adventure makes the river accessible to everyone, creating memories that will last a lifetime!

Classic White Water Adventure

Difficulty: Intermediate | Duration: Full Day (6-8 hours) Ideal For: Adventurers, Intermediate Rafters, Group Outings

Overview

Prepare for a day of action, splashes, and exhilarating fun! The Classic White Water Adventure is our signature trip, designed to offer an authentic rafting experience with a mix of Class II-III rapids. Perfect for guests who want to feel the excitement of white water but at a manageable level, this journey takes you through thrilling rapids and scenic spots where the river widens and slows. Our guides will expertly navigate through the waves, ensuring safety while maximizing the thrill of every rapid.

Highlights

  • Encounter Class II-III rapids that are challenging but manageable
  • Stunning views of forested riverbanks and rocky outcroppings
  • Picnic lunch provided along a scenic riverside spot
  • Opportunities for swimming and cliff-jumping (conditions permitting)

This trip is ideal for those looking to push their boundaries while enjoying the companionship of friends, family, or fellow adventurers on an exciting day-long river journey.

Ultimate White Water Thrill

Difficulty: Advanced | Duration: Full Day (8-10 hours) Ideal For: Thrill-Seekers, Experienced Rafters, Adventure Enthusiasts

Overview

For the daring and seasoned rafter, the Ultimate White Water Thrill is a challenging expedition through Class IV and V rapids, giving you the adrenaline rush you’ve been craving. This high-intensity journey takes you through narrow chutes, powerful waves, and fast-flowing rapids that demand skill and teamwork. Our expert guides will provide thorough safety briefings and advanced paddling instructions to navigate the river’s toughest sections with precision.

Highlights

  • Non-stop action on Class IV and V rapids for serious adrenaline seekers
  • Professional guides equipped with extensive knowledge and advanced techniques
  • Intense rapids interspersed with scenic, quiet pools for breaks and breathtaking views
  • Includes a gourmet riverside lunch to refuel for the journey’s second half

Push your limits and feel the thrill of conquering some of the river’s most powerful rapids. The Ultimate White Water Thrill promises an unforgettable adventure for those who live for the rush and crave the challenge.
