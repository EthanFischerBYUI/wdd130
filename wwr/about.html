History

River Gliding began as a wild dream in the summer of 1985, when three lifelong friends—Jack, Sam, and Lucy—found themselves stranded on the banks of the roaring Thunder River after a camping trip gone wrong. With no way to hike back, they crafted makeshift rafts from fallen trees and braved the rapids to get home. Along the way, they discovered the thrill of white water rafting and an untapped passion for adventure. What started as a daring escape turned into a vision for sharing that excitement with others. By 1986, the trio had officially founded River Gliding, starting with a single raft and the rugged determination to conquer nature’s most challenging rivers.

Word spread quickly about their crazy but exhilarating river runs, and soon, thrill-seekers from across the country flocked to join the ride. Today, River Gliding has grown into a top-rated adventure company, with a fleet of state-of-the-art rafts and experienced guides who share Jack, Sam, and Lucy’s original spirit of daring exploration. From beginners to expert rafters, River Gliding is proud to offer wild and memorable journeys for all!
